Today, students in management schools register for online courses to get a sound understanding of basic concepts in core and elective subjects. The learning grows exponentially in the classroom when faculty do a deep dive into each of the concepts learnt online.

The Faculty Development Programme at IIMB aims to equip faculty with the ability to conduct blended courses that involve combining the online courses with in-class facilitation and teaching.
