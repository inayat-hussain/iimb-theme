The world of business today faces many a challenges. While academic excellence provides students with a knowledge platform and an access to the industry, it does not necessarily prepare aspiring job candidates for the demands of the marketplace. Effective training from the industry perspective can bridge this gap between students and aspiring job candidates. ${_('{platform_name}').format(platform_name=settings.PLATFORM_NAME)} in an effort to blend industrial perspective with theoretical learning has introduced Academic Partnership Programmes.

Academic institutes can partner with ${_('{platform_name}').format(platform_name=settings.PLATFORM_NAME)} to enhance their campus offerings through online programmes designed to integrate with academic curriculum.

Students at undergraduate and graduate levels have an opportunity to gain business and managerial skills along with their conventional academic endeavour. On successful completion of such programmes, students will receive ${_('{platform_name}').format(platform_name=settings.PLATFORM_NAME)} certificates.

Academic Partnership programmes from ${_('{platform_name}').format(platform_name=settings.PLATFORM_NAME)} are offered in two formats.

  • Online certificate programme in Fundamentals of Business Management
  • Specialized Management Courses in a wide range of disciplines
