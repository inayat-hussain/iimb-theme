About ${_('{platform_name}').format(platform_name=settings.PLATFORM_NAME)}

${_('{platform_name}').format(platform_name=settings.PLATFORM_NAME)}, is a programme started in August 2014 by IIM Bangalore with the aim of providing access to world-class education in management to every interested learner in India and across the world. IIMB believes in the disruptive power of technology and is committed to leveraging digital channels to bring our motto of radiant learning to life. The Institute aims to achieve this goal by offering Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) starting from July 2015 through edX, a not-for-profit online initiative of Harvard and MIT that offers online courses from the world's best universities and organizations.

${_('{platform_name}').format(platform_name=settings.PLATFORM_NAME)} Mission:

  • To deliver world-class MOOCs in management to learners across the globe and create positive social impact using educational technology.

${_('{platform_name}').format(platform_name=settings.PLATFORM_NAME)} Goals:

  • To make world-class management education accessible from every part of the world.
  • To empower less privileged learners in India through innovative use of technology.
  • To improve in-classroom teaching and learning.

How ${_('{platform_name}').format(platform_name=settings.PLATFORM_NAME)} benefits learner:

  • Online management courses from IIMB faculty
  • Interactive learning through instructionally designed courses
  • Learning beyond time line boundaries.
  • Learners don't need a degree to follow the course, only the willingness to learn.
  • IIMBx Certificate to enhance professional career

HOW ${_('{platform_name}').format(platform_name=settings.PLATFORM_NAME)} benefits faculty:

  • Provides more advanced technology and tools to the faculty.
  • Provides further pedagogical support with integration of existing resources.
  • Platform is open to experiments for faculty to build broader learner management system.

What ${_('{platform_name}').format(platform_name=settings.PLATFORM_NAME)}is not:

  • It does not offer online diplomas or degrees.
  • Not merely a Learning Management system.
  • Doesn't include static courses.
