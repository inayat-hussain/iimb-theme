The FDP is conducted in a blended fashion with online and face-to-face components.

Online component: Pre-reading is shared in online format 2-3 weeks in advance with an aim to prepare the participants with the right context on how blended online courses with in-class sessions. This also allows participants to have online discussions with fellow faculty before attending the face-to-face session.

Classroom component: The programme is designed to be a day-long event with multiple sessions and tracks. This includes introduction to blended learning pedagogy, blended MOOC orientation sessions, panel discussions, and expert talks.

IIM Bangalore has conducted four Blended MOOC Faculty Development Programmes. More than 400 faculty from over 100 management institutions across India have attended the day-long workshops with an aim to become better digitally equipped faculty.
