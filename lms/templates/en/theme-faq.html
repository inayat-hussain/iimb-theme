${_('{platform_name}').format(platform_name=settings.PLATFORM_NAME)} Basics

Learners enrolled through one of our programmes can take an ${_('{platform_name}').format(platform_name=settings.PLATFORM_NAME)} course.
Interested Institutions or Organizations can email at: digital.learning@iimbx.edu.in.

Courses from ${_('{platform_name}').format(platform_name=settings.PLATFORM_NAME)} will cover a wide range of management disciplines at introductory, intermediate and advanced levels. ${_('{platform_name}').format(platform_name=settings.PLATFORM_NAME)} offers courses on core management topics such as Accounting, Information Systems, Operations Management, Human Relations, Marketing, Strategy, Finance and Statistics, as well as other management topics such as Analytics, Banking and Financial Markets, Information Technology Management, International Business Management and Sustainability.

All login related issues need to be raised with the Programme Co-ordinator, who is assigned for the specific programme.

You need an Internet connection to access the course. The transcripts are downloadable and can be viewed ‘offline’.


Courses

Learners will receive an invitation email to enrol for the course. Once enrolled students can access that particular course.

The course discussion forum is the best place to reach out to the ${_('{platform_name}').format(platform_name=settings.PLATFORM_NAME)} teaching team for your class; just post your questions. We do our best to respond as promptly as possible.

The typical duration of courses from ${_('{platform_name}').format(platform_name=settings.PLATFORM_NAME)} range between 4-7 weeks.

The typical learner effort for a course from ${_('{platform_name}').format(platform_name=settings.PLATFORM_NAME)} is expected to be 5-6 hours per week.

The pass grade for each course is different. This will be clearly stated as part of the “Grading Policy” for the individual course.


Certificates
